Chrome Ore Processing

At TOP FIVE MINING RESOURCES we ensure maximum chrome ore processing, with our well established washing plant. The principle of chrome ore mining process is to maximize the recovery rate of chrome and the associated heavy minerals from Ganges firstly, and then separate chrome ore from the associated heavy minerals by joint mining solutions such as gravity separation, magnetic separation, etc.
